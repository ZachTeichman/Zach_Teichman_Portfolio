About Me

I am a student at the University of Georgia majoring in Finance and Real Estate with a 4.0 GPA. I got interested in finance because I like markets, competition, and work that is actually tied to a real result.

Through Cogent, Corcoran, Orion Society, and Terry Student Consulting, I have been able to see a few different sides of it. I have liked getting early exposure to sourcing work, real estate, consulting-style projects, and team-based pitch work.

Outside of school, I previously coached East Marietta Basketball and played varsity tennis in high school. I am a big Atlanta Hawks and New York Giants fan, like movies, enjoy cooking, and can always make room for buffalo chicken.
